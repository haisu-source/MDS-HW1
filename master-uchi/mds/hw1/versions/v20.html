The Fool is card 0 of the Major Arcana. It represents new beginnings, spontaneity, and a free spirit. The card depicts a young traveler stepping carefree toward the edge of a cliff, carrying a small bundle and a white rose. A small dog follows at their heels. The Fool reminds us to embrace the unknown with an open heart and trust the journey ahead.
